HBR: The Ultimate Humboldt Bay Family Experience!

Our vision is simple. To promote health-conscious, sustainable tourism and economic growth on Humboldt County's expanding 14-mile Bay Trail. Our project is centered around a family-oriented carriage-bicycle rental business that we plan to launch by Summer 2019. Join Us!

About HumBay Rentals

Humbay Rentals invites you and your family to take a self-guided tour of the Humboldt Bay Trail. Beginning Summer 2019, families in the Humboldt County area will have the opportunity to experience the Humboldt County Bay from a firsthand perspective!





Project Details

The value of HumBay Rentals as a community asset can be best assessed with the following criteria in mind:

Health Conscious

The activity-based dynamic of HBR naturally encourages the promotion of a healthy lifestyle.

Sustainable

The HBR project has minimal impact on the environment. In fact, the health of the HBR project will be largely dependant on the maintenance and safety of Humboldt Bay Trail

Accessibility

The HBR project will have adaptive implemenetations accessible to ensure ease of use regardless of handicap, disability, ect.

Tourist Friendly

The placement and presentation of HBR as a centerpiece upon the Humboldt Bay Trail will undoubtedly resonate visually with traveling sightseers from all backgrounds.
