HBR: The Ultimate Humboldt Bay Family Experience!

Our vision is simple. To promote health-conscious, sustainable tourism and economic growth on Humboldt County's expanding 14-mile Bay Trail. Our project is centered around a family-oriented carriage-bicycle rental business that we plan to launch by Summer 2019. Join Us!

About HumBay Rentals

Humbay Rentals was concieved in 2016 as an approach to highlighting Humboldt County's scenic bay view within the context of the expanding tourism insustry.

HumBay Rentals' primary focus is the creation of a sustainable bayfront business that publicly offers pedal-based rentals along the Humboldt Bay Trail, with a focus on carriage bicycle rentals.
