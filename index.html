HumBay Rentals
The Ultimate Humboldt Bay Family Experience!

Our vision is simple. To promote health-conscious, sustainable tourism and economic growth on Humboldt County's expanding 14-mile Bay Trail. Our project is centered around a family-oriented carriage-bicycle rental business that we plan to launch by Summer 2022. Join Us!


About HumBay Rentals...

Humbay Rentals invites you and your family to take a self-guided tour of the Humboldt Bay Trail. Beginning Summer 2022, families in the Humboldt County area will have the opportunity to experience the Humboldt Bay Trail from a firsthand perspective!





Project Details

The value of HumBay Rentals as a community asset can be best assessed with the following criteria in mind:

Activity Based Fun!

The physical aspect of trail exploration activites naturally leads to the promotion of a healthy lifestyle.

Environmentally Sustainable

The health of the HBR project will be largely dependant on the maintenance and safety of Humboldt Bay Trail.

Accessibility

The HBR project will have adaptive implementations available to ensure ease of use regardless of handicap, disability, ect.

Tourist Friendly

The placement and presentation of HBR as a centerpiece upon the Humboldt Bay Trail will undoubtedly resonate visually with traveling sightseers from all backgrounds.

Family Oriented

The nature of carriage bicycle use makes it a perfect vehicle for whole-family engagement.

Multilateral Profits

The HBR project, once established, can act as a "linchpin" organization-acting in conjunction with other businesses/projects in the area.
